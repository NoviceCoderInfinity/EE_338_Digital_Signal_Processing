Obtain the inverse Z-transform of 
\[
\frac{1}{1 - \alpha Z^{-1}}
\]
with Region of Convergence as:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item $|Z| > |\alpha|$
    \item $|Z| < |\alpha|$
\end{enumerate}

Obtain the answer using mathematical induction.
